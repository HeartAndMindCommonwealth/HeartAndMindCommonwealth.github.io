Our Mission

The mission of Heart and Mind Commonwealth is to support families in inspiring our leaders of the future in discovering their God-given mission by instilling a love of learning and a sense of purpose.

We support family centered learning by modeling core values, play, hard work, and preserving constitutional freedom while learning and discussing great classics.

Our principles of education are founded on Leadership Education.

As we collectively come together we build community, serve others, and learn from each other.

About

Heart and Mind Commonwealth is a leadership co-op based on the educational principles of A Thomas Jefferson Education by Oliver DeMille. We are families working together to strengthen heart and mind.
